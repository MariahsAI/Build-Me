FridgeChef — AI Recipe Generator from Fridge Photos

Snap a photo of your fridge. Get dinner ideas in seconds. Stop wasting food, stop wasting time.

Sarah K.
Dec 12, 2025
Consumer AI Mobile
▲ 847 Upvote

The Problem

Every day, 40% of food in American homes goes to waste — $1,500 per household annually. Meanwhile, people spend 30+ minutes deciding what to cook, often giving up and ordering takeout instead. Existing recipe apps require manual ingredient input and return hundreds of irrelevant results. The disconnect between what's actually in your kitchen and what you can make is costing people time, money, and sanity.

The Solution

FridgeChef uses computer vision to instantly identify ingredients from a single photo. Point your phone at your fridge, snap, and get 5 personalized recipe suggestions in under 3 seconds — ranked by how well they match what you have. No typing. No scrolling. No wasted food.

Key Features

  • Instant Photo Recognition — AI identifies 500+ ingredients from fridge/pantry photos with 94% accuracy
  • Smart Recipe Matching — Recipes ranked by ingredient coverage (shows "92% match" vs "67% match")
  • Dietary Intelligence — Remembers your preferences: vegan, gluten-free, kid-friendly, low-carb
  • Expiration Alerts — Flags ingredients going bad soon and prioritizes recipes using them
  • One-Tap Shopping — Missing one ingredient? Add it to your list or order via Instacart
  • Hands-Free Cook Mode — Step-by-step voice guidance while you cook
