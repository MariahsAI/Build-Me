AI Recipe Generator from Fridge Photos

Sarah K. Submitted Dec 12, 2025 Consumer ¬∑ AI ¬∑ Mobile
Upvote

The Problem

Every day, millions of people open their fridge, stare at random ingredients, and have no idea what to make. They end up ordering takeout or letting food go to waste. Meal planning apps exist, but they require you to manually input ingredients and browse through hundreds of recipes that don't match what you actually have.

The Solution

Open the app, snap a photo of your fridge (or pantry), and get instant recipe suggestions based on exactly what's visible. The AI identifies ingredients, suggests 3-5 recipes ranked by how well they match your inventory, and highlights anything you might need to buy. One tap to start cooking with step-by-step instructions.

Key Features

  • Photo Recognition ‚Äî AI identifies ingredients from fridge/pantry photos
  • Smart Matching ‚Äî Recipes ranked by ingredient coverage (90% match vs 60%)
  • Dietary Filters ‚Äî Vegan, gluten-free, low-carb, kid-friendly
  • Waste Tracker ‚Äî Alerts for ingredients about to expire
  • Shopping List ‚Äî One tap to add missing ingredients
  • Cook Mode ‚Äî Hands-free step-by-step with voice
